The Day I Found Out I was a Penn Stater!

Posted on September 19, 2022

Well actually, I should say ‘the week’ I became a Penn Stater. I got my acceptence letter at the same time I recived a couple others in the mail. I was so suprised and so happy that these colleges wanted me at their schools. I didn’t want to make any quick desicions yet since it was rather a big one. I took about a week of contimplating and debating to finally decide on Penn State.
